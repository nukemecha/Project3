Overview


OBJECTIVE


In the project, we analyzed the 2011 lending club loan information to predict future loan approval possibility by creating a machine learning model on categorical data. We analyzed the correlation between different categorical data and loan status, for example, the loan amount, interest rate, annual income. The prediction app is to predict if the applicant could qualify for the loan.
