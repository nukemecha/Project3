Overview


Objective


In this project, we analyzed the 2011 Lending Club loan information to predict the likelihood of the loan being paid off successfully by creating a machine learning model on categorical data.

We analyzed the correlation between different categorical data and loan status, for example, the loan amount, interest rate, annual income. The app predicts if the applicant will successfully pay off the loan based on the initial characteristics of the loan.
